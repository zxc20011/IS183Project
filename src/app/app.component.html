What is WeCare.com?

WeCare Child Care provides early childhood care for any Children who are less than 5 years old and still not attended preschool. Our service with early care and education staff to improve the ability of preschool to apply effective education. The services reduce expulsion and help provide children in need with guidance and support at time when it can be most important for their growth. Upon referral through a licensed child care provider and with parental permission, our Child Care can offer services that include an initial observation and assessment of a child in the child care setting, on-site consultation with staff, brief counseling for the family, support for supervisors and program directors, in-service training for staff members as requested, and referrals to community resources.
